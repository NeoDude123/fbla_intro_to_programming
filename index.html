Welcome to the edge of a magical forest...

How to Play:

Choose an option to progress the story. Your choices shape the adventure. Be careful, as every step along the journey, dangers await! Are you up for the challenge? Any time you wish to back out, you may click "Stop" to exit the forest story. However, those who manage to stay on the path, avoiding all the dangers possible, will be rewarded at the end. Many people however, will not meet such a favorable fate...
